## üåç Key Insights

### By Region
- **Asia**: Fastest absolute growth (15-18% CAGR)
- **Africa**: Fastest relative growth (18-25% CAGR)
- **Europe**: Mature market (8-12% CAGR)

### By Technology
- **Solar**: Fastest growing tech (35-45% CAGR)
- **Wind**: Strong growth (25-30% CAGR)
- **Hydro**: Largest base but maturing (2-4% CAGR)

- **Vietnam**: Fastest growth (35% CAGR)
- **India**: Second-largest growth (22% CAGR)
